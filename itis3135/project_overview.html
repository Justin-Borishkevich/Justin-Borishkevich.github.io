Project Overview

  • Our Clients application will be a public website for their worship group / band. The purpose our application will serve will be providing information about the gorup, relevant links, upcomming events, and a registration form for new members.
  • Our indented users will be from two catagories: People who are interested in joining the team, and people who are interested in a general sense. The poepl who are interested in joining the team will be able to use a sign up form. The people interested in a general sense will be able to see the teams history, upcomming events, and a photo gallery.
  • An Overview of the content of the website is as follows:
    1. A Navigation bar with a Home Page
    2. About Page
    3. Contact Page
    4. Register Page
    5. Gallery Page
    6. Events Page

    There will also be External links to the Church home page and Social Media (most likely in the footer).

Page Design:

  • Name: Home Page

    Purpose: Give a sleak landing page that displays various sections linking to other pages.
    Audience: People who are interested in the ministry
    Content: A list of various sections linking to other pages.
    User Enters Data: No
    Validations: No
    Buttons/Hyperlinks/Drop Downs: Yes
    Actions: Buttons will navigate to other pages or provide link sharing functionality.

  • Name: About Page

    Purpose: Give a short Overview and history of the team, what they do, and their goals.
    Audience: People who are interested in the ministry
    Content: Large amounts of text, and maybe a few images or links to other pages.
    User Enters Data: No
    Validations: No
    Buttons/Hyperlinks/Drop Downs: Yes
    Actions: Hyperlinks to other pages

  • Name: Contact Page

    Purpose: A section that allows a user to find contact information.
    Audience: People who are interested in contacting the ministry
    Content: A list of contact information.
    User Enters Data: No
    Validations: No
    Buttons/Hyperlinks/Drop Downs: Yes
    Actions: Hyperlinks or contact links

  • Name: Register Page

    Purpose: A section that allows a user to sign up for the ministry.
    Audience: People who are interested in joining the ministry
    Content: A form that allows a user to enter information about themselves.
    User Enters Data: Yes
    Validations: Yes
    Buttons/Hyperlinks/Drop Downs: Yes
    Actions: Submit button

  • Name: Gallery Page

    Purpose: A section that allows a user to view photos of the ministry.
    Audience: People who are interested in the ministry
    Content: A gallery of images and image sources.
    User Enters Data: No
    Validations: No
    Buttons/Hyperlinks/Drop Downs: yes
    Actions: Hyperlinks to other pages

  • Name: Events Page

    Purpose: A section that allows a user to view upcoming events.
    Audience: People who are interested in upcoming events
    Content: A list of event sections with dates and times.
    User Enters Data: No
    Validations: No
    Buttons/Hyperlinks/Drop Downs: Yes
    Actions: Hyperlinks to share event information

Dynamic Functionality:

  • I will use javascript for the heading and footer components of all pages. I will also use it for dealing with forms on the registration page, and for all site buttons, link sharing functions, and other interactivity. I will also use it heavily for the gallery and events pages.
